The WAC Clearinghouse, as a Press

Most of Pinakes reads the field one journal at a time. This page does the opposite: it gathers everything the WAC Clearinghouse has put a DOI on — {{ "{:,}".format(overview.total_works) }} works across {{ overview.year_min }}–{{ overview.year_max }}, journals and books together — and asks what a house looks like when you see its whole output at once. No citation graphs live here: the Clearinghouse deposits almost no reference lists, so this is a portrait drawn from authors, editors, affiliations, venues, and the count of who later cited each work.
